This image is about ocean pollution and how garbage affects sea animals. The main subject is a turtle wearing a chip bag as a hat. It holds a trash bag in one hand and a plastic cup in the other, showing how waste has become part of its life. I wanted the scene to look funny but also ironic, to show how strange the ocean has become because of human trash. Around the turtle, there is a crab, a fish, a full trash bin, and plastic bottles to make the sea look messy and polluted.
