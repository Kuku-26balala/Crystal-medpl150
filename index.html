I've always been searching for a way to preserve those fleeting moments in daily life—sunrises and sunsets, sudden rainbows, or a friend's offhand remark. For me, becoming a media creator is about keeping memories that many overlook, transforming them into works that can be revisited.

This is my “digital memory box.” My creative mediums include photographs and short videos, capturing moments from life. The tools I often use are CapCut, Procreate, and social media. My goal is for viewers to resonate with these pieces or find their own fleeting memories while browsing through them.
